Ottessa Moshfegh, 2024

Acrylic on canvas

104 x 104 cm

Add to bags

This geometric painting displays the typographic names of contemporary authors such as Ottessa Moshfegh, combining geometric patterns and color-slat experimentation. It represents a connection between individuals and post-modern literature from a contemporary city perspective.
