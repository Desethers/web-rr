How to be... Millenials ethos, 2023
canvas, pictures from magazines
40 x 100 cm

Add to bag

This geometric painting displays the typographic names of contemporary authors such as Foster Wallace, combining geometric patterns and color-slat experimentation. It represents a connection between individuals and post-modern literature from a contemporary city perspective.
